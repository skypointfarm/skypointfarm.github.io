About Us

Whether you're a beginner looking for lessons or a lifelong rider looking to compete nationally, we've got the resources for you.

Skypoint Farm is a full service Hunter Jumper equestrian facility providing boarding, lessons, training, and sales. We offer: 200’ x 70’ mirrored indoor riding arena with a heated viewing room | 215’ x 115’ all-weather outdoor riding arena | 9 Acre sand/ silt derby field | 30+ acres of open fields for riding and grazing. Skypoint Farm is devoted to creating an educational enjoyable and successful culture. Our focus is on providing top level care and training for our clients and horses while promoting the value of horsemanship, partnership and integrity.

TRAINERS & STAFF

Our staff team is friendly and professional.

REDEAN SHEPPARD

Owner, Coach
Redean has been in the horse business for over 20 years. She has coached IEA teams to Nationals multiple years, and the Kent IHSA team is her third.

Stephanie Keough

Coach, IEA and IHSA
Stephanie coached IEA at Andrews Osborne Academy from 2004-2014 and now serves as the IEA Nationals Coordinator. She coaches the Skypoint IEA team and the Kent State IHSA team.

Alex Shokat

Trainer
Alex Shokat is a USHJA certified horseback riding instructor. Alex has over 10 years of experience as a hunter/jumper trainer and has a successful track record of showing and training students at nationally recognized shows.
